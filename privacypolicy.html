Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Infinity Audiobook. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our collective knowledge services.

2. Data We Collect

  • Identity & Contact: Email address provided during PayPal checkout.
  • Transaction: Payment confirmations via PayPal (we do not see or store your credit card numbers).
  • Technical: IP address and book preferences (Custom ID) used for digital delivery.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly to deliver the audiobook access links, update project progress bars, and send early-access rewards. Your data is processed via automated systems (Make.com and Google Sheets).

4. Third-Party Services

To provide our service, we share necessary data with:

  • PayPal: For secure payment processing.
  • Cloudflare: For website hosting and security.
  • Google: For database management and cloud storage links.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to access, delete, or limit the use of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact our support email.
