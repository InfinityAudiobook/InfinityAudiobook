To ensure the sustainable distribution of high-quality AI-powered knowledge, we utilize a "Digital Credits" system for all community contributions.

Why we use Credits?
  • Privacy & Compliance: Transactions appear as "Digital Credits" on your statement. No specific titles are recorded, protecting your privacy and complying with global payment policies.
  • Collective Sharing: By contributing 100 credits each, a group of 10 pioneers can unlock premium content that would otherwise be costly for an individual.
  • Early Access: As a contributor, you don't need to wait for the 100% goal. You will receive an instant "Priority Unlock" link via email immediately after your contribution.
